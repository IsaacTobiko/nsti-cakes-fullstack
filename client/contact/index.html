Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

We recommend placing your order at least 3–5 days in advance to ensure we have enough time to create your perfect custom cake. For larger or more intricate designs, it’s best to order a week or more ahead.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We provide delivery services within Nairobi and nearby areas. Delivery fees may apply depending on the distance, and we always ensure your cake arrives safely and on time.

Can I customize the cake design and flavours?

Absolutely! You can choose your preferred cake design, flavours, and fillings. We love bringing your creative ideas to life, whether it’s for birthdays, weddings, or special occasions.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary needs, including gluten-free, vegan, and nut-free options. Please let us know your requirements when placing the order so we can ensure your cake is safe and delicious.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods, including cash on delivery, mobile money (M-Pesa), and bank transfers. Payment is usually required at the time of order confirmation.
